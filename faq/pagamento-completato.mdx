## Risposta Gift Card

Hello, and thank you for contacting us. According to our records, your gift card payment has already been processed successfully. Please check your email inbox carefully, including **spam or junk folders**, as the gift card details are usually sent there. If you still do not see it, please wait up to **5 days** before contacting us again.
