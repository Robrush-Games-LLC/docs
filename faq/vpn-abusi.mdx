## Risposta

Hello, thank you for contacting us.

We have reviewed your report and confirmed the **use of a VPN** and other forms of system abuse. Since you have explicitly admitted to this, we will proceed to unblock your account for final verification, but your **coins will be reset** in accordance with our internal policies.

Please note that this type of violation **compromises the fairness of the system** and will not be tolerated. From now on, further violations may result in more severe measures.
