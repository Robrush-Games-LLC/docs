## Risposta breve

Hello, and thank you for contacting us. Offerwall tasks and reward approvals are managed by **external providers**, so we are unable to manually verify or approve those rewards from our side. If you have not received your coins, please contact the offerwall provider directly through their support page or official website and submit your proof there if available.

## Risposta completa

Hello, and thank you for contacting us. Offerwall services are managed by third-party providers, which means we cannot directly control their tracking or manually approve missing rewards. Please contact the relevant offerwall provider through their support channel, and if they confirm the completion but the coins still do not appear in the app, send us a screenshot and we will review whether there may be an app-side issue.
