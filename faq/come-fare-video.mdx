## Risposta

Hello, and thank you for contacting us. To create a sponsored video that meets the minimum requirements, please make sure the video **clearly shows or explains how the RBX Earny app works** and includes the **appropriate hashtags** when required. The content should be **clearly related to the app** and must genuinely promote its features or usage.
